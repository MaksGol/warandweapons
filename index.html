The Spr 300 rifle (special purpose rifle, special purpose rifle) is probably the most specialized weapon on the market today. It is not intended to replace a 308-caliber weapon, but to become an additional sniper weapon. It can be used to neutralize a person or to discreetly destroy street lights, eliminate guard dogs, car tires and other obstacles that the police and Special Forces may encounter in their missions. The 300 Whisper caliber was developed with the aim of creating a high-precision subsonic ammunition that provides a large amount of energy to a firing range of less than 150 meters. A typical warhead weight ranges from 220 to 240 grains. With such a cartridge from the SPR 300, it produces about 40 dB.
